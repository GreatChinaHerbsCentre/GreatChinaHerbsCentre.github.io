Most Popular Products

Goji Berries

Prominently grown in China, Goji berries have been farmed for over 700 years. Goji berries are used to boost the immune system and help with eyesight.

Ginseng

Comes in many forms, Ginseng is widely known to be a healthy energy booster alternative as well as helping to boost memory and the immune system.

Nin Jiom Pei Pa Koa

One of the most popular and well-known traditional Chinese herbal cough syrups, Nin Jiom Pei Pa Koa has been known to be effective for the relief of sore throat, coughs, aphonia and hoarseness.
